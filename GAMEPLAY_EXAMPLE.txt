STRATEGY LESSONS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✓ Collect coins early for economy advantage
✓ Buy abilities before armor (damage > defense early)
✓ Avoid collisions when low HP (instant death)
✓ Multiple bots = map control
✓ Abilities deal safe damage from range
✓ Armor upgrades help survive collisions
✓ Queue movement carefully (2 sec delay)
✓ Spread bots out to cover more area
